# monetization tracker template

use this to track every revenue angle across your work —
active products, affiliate links, services, and ideas in the
pipeline. one file. updated every session when something changes.

the tracker exists so nothing falls through the cracks. a good
idea that never gets a revenue path attached to it stays an idea.
this is where ideas become line items.

---

## template

```markdown
# monetization tracker
last updated: [date]

---

## active products

| product | price | platform | status | notes |
|---------|-------|----------|--------|-------|
| [name] | $[x] | [Gumroad / BMAC / Beehiiv] | live / draft | |

---

## active services

| service | price | link | notes |
|---------|-------|------|-------|
| [name] | $[x] | [direct link] | |

---

## affiliate links

| tool | link | what they get | what you get |
|------|------|--------------|--------------|
| [tool] | [link] | [discount/credits] | [commission] |

---

## pipeline (ideas with revenue potential)

| idea | type | priority | estimated price | next step |
|------|------|----------|----------------|-----------|
| [name] | product / service / guide | H/M/L | $[x] | [action] |

---

## revenue log

| date | source | amount | notes |
|------|--------|--------|-------|
| [date] | [product/affiliate/service] | $[x] | |
```
