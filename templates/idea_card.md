# idea card template

use this template to capture any monetizable idea — a product,
a service, a workflow, a guide. one file per idea. saved in
your vault. linked to a task in your project manager.

the goal of an idea card is to capture everything you know
about an idea at the moment it's live — before the context
fades. a good idea card means you can pick it up six months
later and know exactly what to build.

---

## template

```markdown
# [Idea Name]

**Status:** Captured / Validating / Building / Launched / Paused
**Type:** Digital product / Service / Workflow / Guide / Course
**Priority:** High / Medium / Low
**Source:** [where this idea came from]
**Task ID:** [linked issue in your project manager]

---

## what it is

One paragraph. What this thing is and what it does.
Be specific — not "an AI guide" but "a step-by-step guide
to connecting Obsidian to Claude via MCP, for non-developers."

---

## who it's for

Who specifically would buy or use this.
The more specific the better.

---

## build notes

What would it take to build this?
Tools needed, time estimate, complexity.

---

## content angles

How can this idea generate content before it's a product?
List 2-3 specific post angles.

---

## monetization path

- Price point:
- Where it's sold (Gumroad / BuyMeACoffee / direct):
- Upsell path (what does this lead to):

---

## open questions

What do you still need to figure out before building?

---

## links

- Vault files: [[related file]]
- Task: [project manager link]
```

---

## example

```markdown
# Obsidian + Claude MCP Setup Guide

**Status:** Building
**Type:** Digital product — paid guide
**Priority:** High
**Source:** Repeated questions from audience about how to
             connect Obsidian to Claude
**Task ID:** AUN-201

---

## what it is

A step-by-step guide for non-developers to connect their
Obsidian vault to Claude via MCP. covers local setup on
Mac, hosted setup via Cloudflare Worker for iPhone access,
and the core vault files to build first.

---

## who it's for

Creators and solopreneurs who use Obsidian and Claude but
haven't connected them yet. no coding background assumed.

---

## build notes

Most of the content exists in the vault already.
Needs to be rewritten for a non-technical reader.
Estimated: 3-4 hours to write and format.

---

## content angles

1. "here's how I connected my Obsidian vault to Claude
   in one afternoon" — process post with outcome shown
2. "Claude has no memory by default. here's how I fixed that"
   — problem/solution thread
3. "the setup that makes Claude actually useful for creators"
   — benefit-forward thread

---

## monetization path

- Price: $47
- Sold via: Gumroad
- Upsell: Claude for Creators setup call ($100)

---

## open questions

- Does the guide need video walkthrough or is written enough?
- Host on Gumroad or Beehiiv shop?
```
