# video 🎬

video content in this system covers three distinct workflows:
music videos (Grok Imagine Agent Mode), marketing and product
videos (Higgsfield), and newsletter video overviews (NotebookLM).
each has a different tool set and a different output — but all
of them start with Claude and end with a file ready to post.

---

## tools in this workflow

| tool | role |
|------|------|
| Grok Imagine | music video generation via Agent Mode |
| Higgsfield | product/marketing video, character animation |
| NotebookLM | cinematic video overviews from written content |
| CapCut | stitching clips, final edit |
| Claude | writes storyboards, prompts, and scripts |

---

## music videos — Grok Imagine Agent Mode

this workflow produces a full music video in a single
Grok Imagine conversation using Agent Mode.

**the workflow:**

1. paste your lyrics into Grok Imagine Agent Mode
2. Claude writes a scene-by-scene storyboard first (one scene
   per lyric section, visual direction for each)
3. Grok generates the animated clips scene by scene
4. for instrumental gaps — request B-roll clips that match
   the visual theme
5. download all clips
6. stitch in CapCut — arrange in order, sync to the audio track
7. export and post

**timing:** 30–45 minutes for a full music video end to end.

this is how the BLACK and PINK music videos were built —
both in single Agent Mode conversations.

## marketing and product videos — Higgsfield

Higgsfield's Seedance model generates high-quality AI video
from image or text input. Marketing Studio handles branded
product videos.

**use cases:**
- product showcase videos
- character animation from a reference image
- branded content with consistent visual identity
- short-form video for X, TikTok, Instagram

**the workflow:**

1. upload your reference image or product photo to Higgsfield
2. write a motion prompt — describe what should move, how,
   and in what direction
3. generate via Seedance
4. review and download
5. add audio in CapCut if needed

for soul character training — upload 5-20 reference images
to train a reusable character model. once trained, that
character can be animated consistently across videos.

## newsletter video overviews — NotebookLM

NotebookLM (Google) generates cinematic video overviews from
written content. upload a newsletter issue or article and it
produces a short explainer video with narration and visuals.

**the workflow:**

1. upload your newsletter issue or article to NotebookLM
2. select "Cinematic Video Overview"
3. NotebookLM generates the video
4. download and post to X as a companion to the written piece

**best for:** turning your best newsletter issues into video
content without any manual production work.

requires Google AI Ultra plan for Cinematic Video Overviews.

---

## posting video content

video posts on X follow a different format from text posts —
the video is the hook, the caption is short, and the comment-
pulling question goes in the first reply or in the caption itself.

the caption for a workflow video should show the output,
not describe the process. show what happened — let people
ask how.
