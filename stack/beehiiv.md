# Beehiiv 📬

Beehiiv is a newsletter platform built for growth. it handles
sending, subscriber management, monetization, and analytics
in one place. it has an MCP connector, which means Claude can
read your newsletter data and draft content mid-session.

→ [get Beehiiv](https://www.beehiiv.com/?via=Auny-H) — 20% off your first 3 months

---

## how you can use it

- send newsletters to your subscriber list
- build a free and paid subscriber tier
- sell digital products directly through your newsletter shop
- track open rates, click rates, and subscriber growth
- automate welcome sequences and campaigns
- connect to Claude via MCP for drafting and analytics

---

## setup

1. create an account at [beehiiv.com](https://www.beehiiv.com/?via=Auny-H)
2. set up your publication — name, description, branding
3. import your existing list if you have one
4. enable the Beehiiv MCP in Claude Desktop under
   Settings → Integrations
5. note your publication ID — Claude will need it for MCP calls

## how i use it

my newsletter is called Explained Without Fluff. it runs on Beehiiv.

the Beehiiv MCP connects directly into my Claude sessions.
mid-session Claude can pull subscriber counts, recent post
performance, and shop data without me leaving the conversation.

newsletter content follows a specific flow:
1. Claude reads the EWF newsletter manual from the vault
2. we identify the topic and angle for the issue
3. Claude drafts the full newsletter in the EWF voice and format
4. i review, edit, and schedule in Beehiiv

the newsletter also feeds back into content — EWF issues become
X threads, threads become newsletter topics. the two systems
compound each other.

i also sell digital products through the Beehiiv shop — including
a free 3-part brand kit that functions as a lead magnet for
new subscribers.
