## prompt structure that works

a few patterns that produce consistent results:

**bracket system** — isolate each sound layer in its own bracket.
you're specifying components, not describing a vibe.

**BPM first** — always open with an exact BPM. it sets the
biological anchor before any aesthetic detail.

**quality controls** — define what you don't want:
`no white noise` · `no vocals` · `seamless transitions`
most people only describe what they want. defining the
boundaries of the space produces more precise output.

**emotional + technical hybrid** — mix audio engineering
language with emotional descriptors in the same prompt:
`"slow-burn emotional intensity"` + `"crystalline glitch"` +
`"heartbeat bass"`. the model understands both.
